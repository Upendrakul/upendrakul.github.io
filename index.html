Dr. Upendra Kulshrestha

Bio

I am an Assistant Professor in Automobile Engineering at Manipal University Jaipur, where I have taught and researched since 2013, and I currently also serve as Assistant Director of the Directorate of E-Cell (DoE-Cell). My work sits at the intersection of elastomeric nanocomposites, energy storage and solar energy, and sustainability — from rubber and carbon-nanotube reinforced composites for tyre and automotive applications to flexible polymeric sensors for robotics and rehabilitation.

I completed my Ph.D. in Nanocomposites (2020) at the Department of Mechanical Engineering, Manipal University Jaipur, under the supervision of Prof. N. N. Sharma and co-supervision of Prof. Subrata B. Ghosh. Earlier, I earned an M.Tech. in Thermal Engineering from R.T.U. Kota (2012) and a B.E. in Mechanical Engineering from MITS, Gwalior (2007), and I began my career as a Service Engineer in After-Sales at General Motors, Halol, before moving into teaching in 2002.

Research

Research

My research spans elastomeric nanocomposites for automotive and industrial applications, energy storage and solar energy materials, and sustainability — connecting materials science to real-world vehicle and sensing technologies.

Research Areas

  • Elastomeric nanocomposites (carbon-nanotube, carbon-black, and cellulose-nanofibre reinforced rubber)
  • Energy storage and solar energy (perovskite and lead-free solar cells, sodium-ion battery cathodes)
  • Sustainability
  • Electric vehicle technology
  • Flexible polymeric and photonic-crystal-fibre sensors
